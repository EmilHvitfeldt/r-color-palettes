### Canva palattes

All of these palettes are avaliable with `ggthemes` package
`ggthemes::canva_palettes` and `ggthemes::canva_pal()`.

``` r
source("palette_plotter.R")

make_plot(ggthemes::canva_palettes, "canva")
```
